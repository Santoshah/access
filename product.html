Toxi-Trust

Our Toxi-Trust kit provides everything you need to perform your Drug Screens and/or Confirmation testing. Drug Screening is performed by Immunoassay and all of our testing is run on our Triple Quad LC/MS/MS equipment.

ImmunoDX

ImmunoDX is a tool that leading physicians are using to treat and medically manage patients with arthritis and other rheumatic diseases. With many diseases that are often very complex, Immuno DX helps benefits the patient and physician by looking thoroughly into one's immune status.

Cardio Pro

Cardio Pro is your solution for an Advanced Cardiac Analysis which leading physicians are using for preventive patient care. Physicians nationwide are getting a clear picture of the patient's cardiovascular state by applying these tests to their practice.

Revive

Revive is specifically catered to those that need an advanced heath analysis. From hormones, vitamins, lipids, thyroid, organ functions, Revive will give you insight on your health. All tests are resulted within 24hrs giving the physician the proper data needed to  asses a patients well being and diagnose effectively in a timely manner. 

Imatrix

Imatrix sets the benchmark for Salivary assays. This product line caters to those that need a quick, accurate, non invasive method for measuring hormone levels and adrenals. Being the ONLY lab in the nation to perform these tests on LC/MS/MS, we assure you the most accurate, efficient and effective results in the industry.

The Female Evaluator

The Female EvaluatoR can be used in the investigation of problems such as functional inferility, ovulation problems, hot flashes, hirsutism, recurring headaches, libido problems, sexual differentiation problems, and estrogen - progesterone imbalances.

The Hormone Blue Print

The Hormone BluePrint is an extensive 4 Day Assessment monitoring salivary hormone levels in order to get a clearer picture of the Patients health.
